# Health Plan Prior Authorization Problem Statement

**Type:** Problem Statement
**Domain:** Healthcare Operations
**Context:** Regional Health Plan Prior Authorization Process

## Summary

A regional health plan processes approximately 15,000 prior authorization requests per month with manual nurse review. The current process has significant inefficiencies: 4.2-day average turnaround time, 22% rework rate due to missing information, and frequent provider status calls. Leadership aims to reduce turnaround time by 40% without increasing headcount while maintaining clinical quality and regulatory compliance.

## Key Takeaways

- **Scale:** 15,000 prior authorization requests per month
- **Current process:** Manual nurse review of clinical documentation from multiple sources (fax, PDFs, portals, provider uploads)
- **Performance metrics:** 4.2-day average turnaround time, 22% rework rate
- **Pain points:** Missing information causing rework, provider status inquiry calls
- **Goal:** 40% reduction in turnaround time without headcount increase
- **Constraints:** Must maintain clinical quality and regulatory compliance
