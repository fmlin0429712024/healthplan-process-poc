# Claude

**Type:** AI Assistant/Platform
**Developer:** Anthropic
**Category:** Large Language Model

## Summary

Claude is an AI assistant developed by Anthropic, designed to be helpful, harmless, and honest. It is being considered as a solution for healthcare workflow automation, specifically for prior authorization process improvement. Claude offers capabilities in document understanding, clinical reasoning, and workflow integration that could address operational challenges in healthcare settings.

## Capabilities

**Document Processing:**
- Multi-format document understanding (PDFs, faxes, portals)
- Clinical information extraction
- Completeness assessment
- Guideline verification

**Workflow Integration:**
- Coworker-style collaboration patterns
- Human-in-the-loop workflows
- Escalation and review processes
- Status communication automation

**Clinical Reasoning:**
- Medical guideline interpretation
- Clinical appropriateness assessment
- Documentation synthesis
- Decision support

## Healthcare Considerations
