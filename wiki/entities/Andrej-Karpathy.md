# Andrej Karpathy

**Type:** Person
**Role:** AI Researcher, Educator
**Organization:** OpenAI (former), Tesla (former)

## Summary

Andrej Karpathy is a prominent AI researcher and educator known for his work on deep learning, computer vision, and large language models. He authored the LLM Wiki pattern in 2026, proposing a new approach to personal knowledge management using LLMs as persistent wiki maintainers.

## Contributions

- **LLM Wiki Pattern (2026):** Proposed the pattern of using LLMs to incrementally build and maintain persistent wikis, addressing the maintenance burden that causes humans to abandon knowledge bases.

- **Deep Learning Education:** Known for clear, accessible explanations of complex AI concepts through courses, tutorials, and blog posts.
