# Regional Health Plan

**Type:** Organization
**Sector:** Healthcare Insurance
**Scope:** Regional operations

## Summary

Regional health plans are insurance organizations that provide healthcare coverage to specific geographic areas. They process prior authorization requests, manage provider networks, and oversee clinical documentation review processes. These organizations face operational challenges around manual workflows, regulatory compliance, and provider satisfaction.

## Operational Context

**Scale:** Typically process thousands of prior authorization requests monthly (e.g., 15,000/month for the case study)

**Key Processes:**
- Prior authorization review and approval
- Clinical documentation management
- Provider communications and status updates
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Network management

**Common Challenges:**
- Manual review workflows
- Multi-channel documentation intake
- Turnaround time pressures
- Rework due to missing information
- Provider inquiry volume
- Balancing efficiency with clinical quality
