# Interview Assignment Deliverables

**Assignment:** Design and develop a rapid AI prototype of Claude for health plan prior authorization process
**Presentation Format:** 15 minutes
**Target Audience:** Chief Medical Officer and leadership
**Scope:** Claude Code/coworker desktop prototype (NOT enterprise cloud deployment)

## Deliverable #1: 3 AI Opportunities Analysis

**What:** Analysis of 3 distinct AI opportunities for the prior authorization problem space

**Requirements:**
- Address the specific problem: 15,000 requests/month, 4.2-day turnaround, 22% rework rate
- Focus on improving the process while maintaining clinical quality and regulatory compliance
- Business-friendly explanation (not overly technical)
- Each opportunity should be clear, actionable, and relevant to the CMO's question

**Format:** Presentation slides or structured document suitable for 15-minute delivery

## Deliverable #2: Claude Coworker Prototype

**What:** Concrete demonstration of Claude's collaborative AI capabilities applied to prior authorization

**Requirements:**
- Desktop-based prototype using Claude Code/coworker features
- Demonstrate Claude's ability to assist with the prior authorization workflow
- Show concrete examples of Claude helping with:
  - Document processing and understanding
  - Clinical guideline verification
  - Missing information identification
  - Workflow assistance
- Working demo/prototype (not just screenshots)

**Platform:** Claude Code/coworker desktop environment

## Deliverable #3: 15-Minute Presentation

**What:** Complete presentation covering all deliverables

**Requirements:**
- Business-friendly format for CMO/leadership audience
- Time: 15 minutes total
- Structure:
  - Problem statement summary
  - 3 AI opportunities
  - Claude coworker prototype demonstration
  - Extensibility architecture explanation
  - Conclusion/recommendations

**Format:** Presentation slides with live prototype demonstration

## Deliverable #4: Extensibility Architecture

**What:** Business-friendly explanation of how the solution could scale

**Requirements:**
- Conceptual architecture (not technical implementation details)
- Show understanding of enterprise considerations
- Explain how the Claude coworker approach could extend to:
  - Other healthcare workflows
  - Larger scale operations
  - Different use cases
- Business-friendly language suitable for leadership audience

**Format:** Architecture diagram/explanation within the presentation

## Success Criteria

**Must Have:**
- ✅ 3 clear AI opportunities relevant to the problem
- ✅ Working Claude coworker prototype demonstration
- ✅ Complete 15-minute presentation
- ✅ Extensibility explanation in business terms
- ✅ Addresses CMO's question about AI + clinical quality + compliance

**Nice to Have:**
- 🎯 Specific metrics/ROI estimates
- 🎯 Implementation timeline considerations
- 🎯 Risk mitigation strategies
- 🎯 Competitive landscape context

## Timeline Considerations

**Focus:** Rapid prototype approach - speed over perfection
**Quality:** Demonstrate capability and problem-solving, not production-ready solution
**Audience:** Interview assessment - show thinking process and AI understanding
